This webpage focuses on border collies and the issue of their high dumping rate. I started by introducing their key characteristics, such as high intelligence and energy, then gradually connects these traits to a deeper issue—many owners underestimate the time and effort required, which can lead to higher abandonment rates. From there, the website expands the topic to the broader situation of shelter animals in the United States, using data visualizations to show intake and adoption trends. Finally, it highlights the importance of responsible pet ownership and animal protection.
